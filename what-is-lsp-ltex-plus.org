** lsp-ltex-plus is here to help!
This package brings the power of LanguageTool directly into your Emacs buffer. It is a "add-on" server that runs quietly in the background, catchin errors as you type.

- It works with *Markdown*, *LaTeX*, and *Org-mode*.
- It is fully **offline** (no one else sees your text!).
- It is built for researchers, scholars, and writers in the humanities.



Don't let a small typo's ruin your brilliant idears. Try lsp-ltex-plus today and see the differance!
